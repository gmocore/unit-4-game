Idris Elba rapping is the best thing to happen this week

By Naina Aidasani 4 months, 1 week

The week is not even close to ending yet, but I already know what its highlight is going to be: Idris Elba rapping.

People Magazine's 'Sexiest Man Alive' is one with countless talents; we’re just thanking our stars that we get to see him doing so many different things at one time.

Here is his latest attempt at breaking the music scene. If there’s better eye candy out there, do let me know:

How so cool, Mr. Elba?

This isn’t all that this gorgeous man has been up to because 2019 is turning out to be the year Elba shines bright.

After recently appearing on SNL, Elba is set to headline this year’s Coachella.

His Netflix series, Turn Up Charlie, in which he stars and directs, is set to premiere on March 15. If all of this wasn’t enough, he is currently filming alongside his star studded ensemble the remake for Cats with Taylor Swift, Rebel Wilson and many others.

Music, acting, directing, rapping, seems like there is nothing that the Luther actor cannot do. When asked who inspires him, Elba looks up to Donald Glover and hopes to follow his career graph. Safe to say, he’s on his way.

Seriously, does the man ever sleep?

And while Elba may have confirmed to the world that he has not been formally approached by the James Bond franchise, we are praying that this somehow becomes reality.
